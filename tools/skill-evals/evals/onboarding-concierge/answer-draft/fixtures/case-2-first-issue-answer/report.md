## Your first contribution

The best place to start is the filtered
[good-first-issue list](https://github.com/example/acme/issues?q=label%3A%22good+first+issue%22).
These issues are small, self-contained, and have enough context that a
new contributor can work on them without knowing the whole codebase.

To claim an issue, leave a comment on it saying you'd like to work on
it. A maintainer will assign it to you or confirm it is still available.
Do not open a PR for an unclaimed issue — another contributor may
already be working on it.

Once you have an assignment, follow the workflow in the
[Making changes](making-changes) section.
